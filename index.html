Hello! I'm Freya (Henry). I'm a digital hardware, 'wearables' and general engineering nerd. I work at UW-Madison right now as a research intern (pre-grad-student? Candidate? who knows) studying low energy IoT sensing platforms for agriculture.

ECE 554 Senior Design

As part of finishing up my B.S. in Computer Engineering, I did a Senior Design/Capstone project (ECE554). A major part of my team's project was gesture acceleration so I designed a hardware-accelerated image downscaling pipeline that was... a really doozy. Uses a parameterizable (try saying that few times fast), self-pipelining adder tree to interpolate and downscale live frames.

Proot HUD (Old)
