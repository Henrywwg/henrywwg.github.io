This was a semester long digital design project I did for one of my undergrad ECE courses. There was quite a bit of depth and breadth in this, touching on communication protocols, a lot of state machine and processor design, working with memory and a bit of interfacing with analog components as well.

Pictured above is the logic analyzer implemented onto a DE0-NANO FPGA, triggering on some UART or SPI signals.

Designed following the guidelines of bettermfwebsite.com
